Mora

MSRP 2.269,00 €
Includes two magazines, M-Lok bottle shield, carbon bottle, Hušh 50 carbon silencer, without scope and bipod.

In Slovene mora refers to a "nightmare". Mora is one of the spirits from ancient Slav mythology. Mora was a dark spirit that takes a form of a beautiful woman and then visits men in their dreams, torturing them with desire, and dragging life out of them.

Multi Calibers

Mora is available in 4.5 (.177), 5.5 (.22), 6.35 (.25), 7.62 (.30), 9 (.35) calibers and arrow barrel.

What's included?

Two magazines, carbon bottle and carbon shroud comes in as standard. In Compact and Standard configuration, M-Lok bottle shield is included.

Mora comes with an industry grade heavy duty transport hard case with hard foam cutout with plenty of room.

New RPB Barrels

Mora in cal .22 and .25 is equipped with RTI's Proprietary Barrel which assures superb accuracy with pellets and slugs. Barrels are compatible with P-3 platform.

M-Lok compatible holes

Mora's Breech block is equipped with M-Lok compatible holes one can mount different M-Lok accessories such as picatinny rail. This feature can also be found under the trigger guard.

125ccm Plenum

Breech block with integrated 125 ccm Plenum together with the new externally adjustable Assisted valve gives great energy output and efficiency.

Adjustable Assisted Valve

Mora comes with the externally adjusted assisted valve system which uses air from the barrel to close faster. Rapid opening and closing helps with great energies at low pressure and great efficiency. Due to the valve being externally adjustable, dwell time can be adjusted for best performance.

Externally Adjustable Regulator

Mora comes with the twin regulation. Second stage is externally adjustable. It can regulate from 50 bar to 200 bar.

Mora Compact

Mora Compact comes with 500mm/20" barrel, 500ccm carbon fiber bottle, two magazines, Hušh 50 carbon silencer, M-Lok bottle shield and heavy duty hard case.

Mora Standard

Mora Standard comes with 600mm/24" barrel, 700ccm carbon fiber bottle, two magazines, Hušh 50 carbon silencer, M-Lok bottle shield and heavy duty hard case.

Mora Sniper

Mora Sniper comes with 700mm/28" barrel, 1100ccm carbon fiber bottle, two magazines, Hušh 50 carbon silencer and heavy duty hard case.

Integrally Suppressed

Mora is integrally suppressed (for the markets where it is allowed) with the monocore design. Mora also comes with the additional suppressor already included, for those who want to be extra quiet.

Truly Free Floating Barrel Optional

We have designed Mora with great accuracy in mind. The barrel can either be truly free floating or it can be locked in place below the scope area. What ever suits your needs.

Witness Holes

Mora includes a smart feature where each hole corresponds to one specific oring in the rifle. If there is a leak, you can detect exactly which oring is leaking from the outside.

Valve on Mora is set in such a way, so it can easily be removed without disassembling anything else on the rifle. You can easily take out the whole valve assembly.

Inter-changeable Calibers

Calibers can be easily and quickly interchanged.

Ambidextrous Mag

Smart design of a magazine allows it to be rotated to the other side so it is lefty or righty friendly.

Suppressor ready & included

Mora is supressor ready and also comes with Hušh 50 Carbon Suppressor already included.

Adjustable Trigger

First and second stage of the trigger can be easily adjusted down to 200g.

Heavy Duty Case

Mora comes with an industry grade heavy duty transport hard case with hard foam cutout with plenty of room for accessories.
