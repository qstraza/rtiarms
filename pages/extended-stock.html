Extended Stock with Adjustable Hammer Spring Screw

In order to offer the best performance we developed an extended stock which gives ability to adjust tension of the hammer spring, so you are able to adjust the velocity to your likings.

Extended Stock works great together with Externally Adjustable Pressure Regulator so optimal performance can be achieved.
